# EDA Report – Bank Loan Dataset

## 📊 Overview
Performed Exploratory Data Analysis on bank loan dataset to understand patterns, trends, and relationships.

---

## 🔢 Summary Statistics
- Total Loan Applications: 0.4K
- Total Funded Amount: $4.9M
- Total Amount Received: $5.4M
- Average Interest Rate: 11.5%
- Average DTI: 14.3

---

## 📈 Key Insights

1. Majority of loans fall under:
   - Loan Status: Fully Paid / Current

2. Good vs Bad Loans:
   - Good Loans %: 89.8
   - Bad Loans %: 10.2

3. Monthly Trend:
   - Highest applications observed in December 

4. State-wise Analysis:
   - State with highest loans: CA 

5. Purpose Analysis:
   - Most common loan purpose: Debt consolidation

- Bar charts (State-wise loans)

## 📌 Conclusion
EDA helped identify trends in loan distribution, repayment behavior, and risk patterns.
